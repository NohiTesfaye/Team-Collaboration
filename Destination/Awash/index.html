Welcome to Awash National Park

Awash National Park is located in the Great Rift Valley and is home to a variety of wildlife, including oryx, kudus, baboons, and over 450 species of birds. The park is also famous for its dramatic landscapes and the Awash River.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Awash National Park is during the dry season, from October to March, when the weather is pleasant, and wildlife is easier to spot.
