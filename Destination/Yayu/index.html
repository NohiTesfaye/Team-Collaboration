Welcome to Yayu Forest

Yayu Forest is one of the last remaining wild coffee forests in the world. It is home to a variety of wildlife, including monkeys, birds, and rare plant species. The forest is a haven for eco-tourists and nature lovers.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Yayu Forest is during the dry season, from November to March, when the weather is pleasant, and the trails are easier to navigate.
