Welcome to Bale Mountain National Park

Bale Mountain National Park is one of the most beautiful and diverse parks in Ethiopia. It is home to unique wildlife, including the Ethiopian wolf, and offers stunning landscapes ranging from lush forests to alpine meadows.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Bale Mountain National Park is during the dry season, from November to March. During this time, the weather is pleasant, and wildlife is easier to spot.
