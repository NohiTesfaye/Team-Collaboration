Welcome to Dhati Walal

Dhati Walal is a pristine natural area located in the Oromia region of Ethiopia. It is known for its breathtaking landscapes, diverse wildlife, and tranquil environment, making it an ideal destination for eco-tourism.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Dhati Walal is during the dry season, from November to March, when the weather is pleasant, and the trails are accessible.
