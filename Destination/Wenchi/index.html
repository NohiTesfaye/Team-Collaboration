Wenchi Crater Lake

Wenchi Crater Lake is a stunning natural wonder located in the Oromia region of Ethiopia. Surrounded by lush greenery and hot springs, it's a perfect destination for hiking and relaxation.

Welcome to Wenchi Crater Lake

Wenchi Crater Lake is one of the most beautiful destinations in Ethiopia. The lake is located inside a volcanic crater and is surrounded by breathtaking landscapes, making it a must-visit for nature lovers.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Wenchi Crater Lake is during the dry season, from October to March. During this time, the weather is pleasant, and the trails are easier to navigate.
